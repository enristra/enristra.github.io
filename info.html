About Me

How it started

My passion for software engineering blossomed in middle school when I struggled to find a note-taking app that suited my needs. Existing apps were either overloaded with features or targeted towards business users. This frustration sparked a desire to create my own, user-friendly apps.

Intrigued by how these applications functioned, I delved into the world of software development. Determined to understand the intricacies of coding, I began learning programming fundamentals alongside my demanding high school curriculum.

The more I learned about software development, the more fascinated I became by its potential to solve problems and create innovative solutions. It became increasingly clear that computer engineering was the perfect path to bring my programming aspirations to life.

Where I'm at

I'm currently pursuing a master's degree in Computer Engineering in Bologna. My interests span full-stack development, systems programming, and the intersection of software with real-world problems.

Outside of university, I build side projects — from data-driven apps to tools I wish existed. I also enjoy football, keeping up with technology, and occasionally writing about things I learn.
